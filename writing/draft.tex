\subsection{Coarse-grained models}
Coarse grained molecular dynamic models like the MARTINI model are a very 
effective to simulate large molecular systems. Unlike all atom simulation 
which aim to simulate the trajectories of every atom, the idea is to 
substitude groups of atoms by a bead which is given properties that match 
the corresponding atoms.
